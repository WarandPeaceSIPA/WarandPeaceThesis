Research Hypotheses

H1

Information Quality Hypothesis

The quality and accuracy of information significantly impacts the effectiveness of structural constraints in preventing conflict.

H2

Communication Channel Hypothesis

Effective communication channels between actors enhance the constraining power of structural factors in international relations.

H3

Interactive Effect Hypothesis

The interaction between information availability and communication effectiveness creates synergistic effects on structural constraint outcomes.

H4

Historical Context Hypothesis

Historical precedents and contextual factors moderate the relationship between informational conditions and structural constraint effectiveness.
